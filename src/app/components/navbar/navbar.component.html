Instructions To Follow

  1. As you logged in you see Dashboard with brief information about invoices & sale details.

  2. To proceed first you have to Add Customer/Client or (Customers / Clients).

  3. After adding customers next step is to Add Product which contains two steps:

    1. First you have to Add Category for this product.

    2. After adding category you can add product.

      • *If you are adding to existing product then no need of adding category just select Product Name.

  4. Now you are ready to make invoices.

  5. At Invoice page make sure that you have addded all required things & Enter details for making invoice.

  6. After making invoice you will redirected to generated invoice details page.

  7. On Invoice details page you see generated invoice & that you can print from here.
